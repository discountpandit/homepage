DiscountPandit

DiscountPandit is a deal search engine that helps you to find the best deals in your neighborhood. With deal sites mushrooming in all parts of the world, we felt the need to provide our consumers an easy access to all relevant deals. We provide you a simpler, faster and a more comprehensive approach to search for deals.

Our algorithm indexes deals from a wide range of deal sources- daily deal sites, group buying sites, deal aggregators, ecommerce sites, etc. We also index deals collected from a plethora of offline sources like newspapers, radio ads, hoardings, etc. thereby ensuring that your deal search is relevant, comprehensive and complete.

In our new version, we have incorporated some new features like Deal Alerts, Follow Merchants and Follow Brands to help you stay tuned to the latest deals on DiscountPandit. We aim to personalize the whole experience of your deal search!
